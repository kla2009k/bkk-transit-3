# Bangkok Transit 3D

## Problem
Passengers do not only need a map. They need confidence: where to board, where to transfer, how much to pay, whether the 20-baht scheme applies, and how to exit the station.

## Solution
Bangkok Transit 3D is a browser-based 3D rail assistant that combines:

- Real 3D network map.
- Route planner with transfer steps.
- 20-baht fare comparison.
- Service time and train headway.
- First Ride Simulator.
- MRTA Mode for Blue, Purple, Yellow, and Pink lines.
- Data-source transparency.

## Why It Matters For MRTA
- Helps new users understand rail trips before travelling.
- Makes MRTA lines easier to present in a connected network.
- Supports the 20-baht policy with route-level comparison.
- Opens a path to official incident, accessibility, and service APIs.

## Evidence In The Prototype
- 10 rail lines rendered from real map geometry.
- Fare comparison is route-aware.
- PWA/static app can run locally and offline after caching.
- Data status labels distinguish official, open, estimated, and local data.

## Recommended Demo Flow
1. Open app.
2. เพิ่มเติม → Demo Mode → เริ่มเดโม.
3. บริการ → เทียบราคา 20฿ vs ปกติ.
4. บริการ → ซ้อมนั่งครั้งแรก.
5. เพิ่มเติม → ที่มาข้อมูลแบบละเอียด.
